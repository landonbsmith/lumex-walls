Salt Lake City LED Wall Installation Process

1. Free Consultation

We visit your Salt Lake City home or business to assess the space, discuss your goals, and take measurements. We evaluate viewing distances, ambient light, structural considerations, and power availability. Available evenings and weekends for busy professionals.

2. Custom Design & Quote

Based on your consultation, we create a detailed design including pixel pitch recommendation, wall dimensions, mounting solution, and smart home integration plan. Transparent pricing with no surprises. We work with your timeline and budget.

3. Professional Installation

Our Utah-based installation team handles everything — structural mounting, electrical, panel installation, calibration, and system programming. We understand Salt Lake City building codes and HOA requirements. Most installations complete in 1-3 days.

4. Local Support & Service

We're headquartered in St. George but serve the entire Wasatch Front regularly. Local technical support, maintenance plans, and warranty service. We schedule service trips to Salt Lake City weekly — you're never waiting weeks for support.
