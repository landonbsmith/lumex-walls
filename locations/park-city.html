Park City LED Wall Installation Process

1. Free On-Site Consultation

We visit your Park City home or business to assess the space, discuss your vision, and take measurements. We understand mountain architecture — from timber frame to modern mountain contemporary. We evaluate viewing distances, ambient light, structural considerations, and power availability.

2. Custom Luxury Design

Based on your consultation, we create a detailed design including pixel pitch recommendation, wall dimensions, mounting solution, and smart home integration plan. For Park City properties, we focus on aesthetics and seamless integration with your mountain home's design. Transparent pricing with no surprises.

3. Professional Mountain Installation

Our experienced installation team handles everything — structural mounting (critical in mountain homes with unique framing), electrical, panel installation, calibration, and system programming. We understand Park City building codes and HOA requirements. Most installations complete in 1-3 days.

4. Ongoing Support & Maintenance

We provide ongoing technical support, maintenance plans, and warranty service. We schedule regular service trips to Park City and can respond quickly when you need assistance. Seasonal maintenance for outdoor displays ensures optimal performance year-round.
