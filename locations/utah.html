Utah LED Wall Installation Process

1. Free In-Home Consultation

We visit your Utah home or business to assess the space, discuss your goals, and take measurements. We evaluate viewing distances, ambient light, structural considerations, and power availability.

2. Custom Design & Quote

Based on your consultation, we create a detailed design including pixel pitch recommendation, wall dimensions, mounting solution, and smart home integration plan. Transparent pricing with no surprises.

3. Professional Installation

Our Utah-based installation team handles everything — structural mounting, electrical, panel installation, calibration, and system programming. Most installations complete in 1-3 days.

4. Local Support & Service

We're here when you need us. Local technical support, maintenance plans, and warranty service. No waiting for out-of-state companies to fly someone out — we're right here in Utah.
