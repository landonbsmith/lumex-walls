Utah LED Wall Installation Process

1. Free In-Home Consultation

We visit your Utah home or business to assess the space, discuss your goals, and take measurements. We evaluate viewing distances, ambient light, structural considerations, and power availability. Based in St. George, we serve the entire state with scheduled consultation trips.

2. Custom Design & Quote

Based on your consultation, we create a detailed design including pixel pitch recommendation, wall dimensions, mounting solution, and smart home integration plan. Transparent pricing with no surprises. We design for Utah's unique climate — from desert heat to mountain cold.

3. Professional Installation

Our Utah-based installation team handles everything — structural mounting, electrical, panel installation, calibration, and system programming. We understand local building codes across Utah's municipalities. Most installations complete in 1-3 days.

4. Local Support & Service

We're right here in St. George — not an out-of-state company. Local technical support, maintenance plans, and warranty service. No waiting for someone to fly in. We schedule regular service trips across the state and can respond quickly when you need us.
