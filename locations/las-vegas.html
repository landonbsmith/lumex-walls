Las Vegas-Specific Considerations

Extreme Heat Performance

Las Vegas summers are brutal on electronics. We specify LED walls with operating ranges up to 140°F, advanced thermal management, and enhanced cooling systems. Your display performs flawlessly even when it's 115°F outside.

Desert Dust Protection

Monsoon season brings dust storms that can damage electronics. Our outdoor installations feature IP65-rated sealed cabinets with enhanced dust filtration. Your LED wall stays clean and functional through any dust event.

Union Labor Coordination

Working in casinos and convention centers often requires union labor. We have experience coordinating with IATSE and other unions to ensure smooth installations that meet venue requirements.

High-End Aesthetics

Vegas is all about presentation. We design installations that look as good as they perform. Hidden wiring, custom mounting solutions, and seamless integration with your interior design. Your LED wall enhances your space, even when it's off.

Las Vegas LED Wall Installation Process

1. On-Site Consultation

We visit your Las Vegas location to assess the space, discuss your vision, and take precise measurements. We evaluate viewing angles, ambient light (critical in our desert environment), and structural requirements.

2. Custom Design & Proposal

Detailed design including pixel pitch selection, wall dimensions, mounting solution, and integration plan. Transparent pricing with no hidden fees. We work with your budget and timeline.
