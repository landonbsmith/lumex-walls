St. George LED Wall Installation Process

1. Free Local Consultation

We're right here in St. George — we'll visit your home or business within 24-48 hours. We assess your space, discuss goals, take measurements, and evaluate viewing distances, ambient light (especially important in our bright desert sun), and power availability.

2. Custom Design & Quote

Based on your consultation, we create a detailed design including pixel pitch recommendation (critical for outdoor desert installations), wall dimensions, mounting solution, and smart home integration plan. Transparent pricing with no surprises.

3. Professional Installation

Our St. George-based installation team handles everything — structural mounting, electrical, panel installation, calibration, and system programming. We understand local building codes and HOA requirements. Most installations complete in 1-3 days.

4. Local Support & Service

We're headquartered in St. George — we're not flying someone in from Salt Lake. Fast local technical support, maintenance plans, and warranty service. Need help? We're minutes away, not hours. Same-day service when you need it.
