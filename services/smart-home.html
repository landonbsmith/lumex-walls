Smart Home LED Wall Integration

Your LED video wall shouldn't exist in isolation. We integrate it seamlessly with your smart home automation system so everything works together — lighting, audio, shades, climate, and your stunning display, all controlled from one interface.

Discuss Your Integration

Why Integration Matters

A beautiful LED wall is only half the experience. Smart integration makes it effortless.

🎬

One-Touch Scenes

"Movie Night" dims the lights, closes the shades, turns on the LED wall, and sets the audio to surround mode — all with a single button press. No juggling five different remotes.

🎤

Voice Control

"Hey, show me the game on the big screen." Voice integration with your smart home system means you can control your LED wall hands-free from anywhere in the room.

📱

Remote Access

Control your LED wall from anywhere with your phone. Turn it on before you get home, schedule content, or check that everything's running — all from your smart home app.

🔗

Unified Control

Your LED wall, whole-home audio, lighting, climate, security — all on one interface. No more learning multiple apps or keeping track of different remotes for each system.

⏰

Automated Schedules

Set your LED wall to display artwork during the day, switch to sports mode at 5pm, and dim to ambient visuals at bedtime. Automation handles it without lifting a finger.

🏠

Whole-Home Sync

Extend what's playing on your main LED wall to other rooms. Start a movie in the theater and continue in the kitchen. Your content follows you throughout the house.

Platforms We Integrate With

Expert integration with all major smart home and automation systems

Control4

As certified Control4 integrators, we create seamless LED wall experiences that work perfectly with your existing Control4 ecosystem. From touch panels to voice control, your LED wall becomes a first-class citizen in your smart home.

Savant

Savant's premium automation platform deserves a premium display experience. We integrate LED walls with Savant's intuitive interface, creating scenes and automations that make your technology disappear into the experience.

Crestron

For enterprise and high-end residential Crestron installations, we ensure your LED wall integrates with existing Crestron control systems. Reliable, scalable, and built for demanding environments.

Other Platforms

RTI, Elan, Josh, Home Assistant, and more. If you have an automation platform, we can integrate your LED wall into it. Our team has experience with virtually every major smart home system on the market.
