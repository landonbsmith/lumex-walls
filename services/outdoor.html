Built for the Elements

Engineered to thrive in Utah and Nevada's demanding outdoor conditions

Weather-Resistant Design

IP65-rated protection against dust, rain, snow, and humidity. Sealed cabinets and corrosion-resistant materials ensure your investment lasts for years, not months. Built to handle everything from monsoon season to winter snow.

Extreme Temperature Performance

Operating range from -20°F to 140°F. Whether it's a Utah winter or a Las Vegas summer (110°F+), your LED wall performs consistently. Advanced thermal management prevents overheating in direct sun.

High Brightness

5,000-7,000 nit brightness levels ensure your display is visible even in direct sunlight. No more washed-out images or squinting to see the score. Crystal-clear visuals at any time of day.

UV & Fade Resistance

Specialized coatings protect against UV damage and color fading. Your LED wall maintains vibrant colors and brightness year after year, even with constant sun exposure.

Wind & Structural Integrity

Engineered mounting systems rated for high winds. We assess your location's wind exposure and design mounting solutions that keep your display secure in any conditions.

Easy Maintenance

Front-serviceable design means no climbing ladders or removing the entire wall for maintenance. Quick access to components for cleaning, repairs, or upgrades.

Utah vs. Las Vegas: Climate Considerations

Utah Mountain Climate

Cold winters with snow, hot summers, and significant temperature swings. We specify displays rated for freeze-thaw cycles and snow loads. Heating elements prevent ice buildup on the display surface.

Las Vegas Desert Climate

Extreme heat (110°F+), intense UV exposure, and monsoon dust storms. We select high-temperature rated panels with advanced cooling systems and enhanced dust protection for desert conditions.
